Absolutely! We designed this program for digital nomad parents and teens who are new to online business. We start from the basics and guide you through every step.

No problem! Week 1 is dedicated to helping you brainstorm and develop profitable business ideas using AI tools and market research techniques.

We recommend 5-10 hours per week to complete the modules and participate in the community. The program is designed to fit around your travel schedule.

Can my teen participate with me?

Yes! We encourage family participation. Teens can learn alongside parents, and we have specific content and activities designed for different age groups.

What kind of support do you provide?

You'll have access to our private community, weekly coaching calls, and direct support from our team. Plus, you can connect with other families in the program.
