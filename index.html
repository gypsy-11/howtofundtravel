From Dream to Reality

Hi there! I'm Melissa, the founder of How to Fund Travel. Buckle up, because my journey to get here has been anything but ordinary.

It all started with a dream: selling everything, traveling the world, and living a location-independent life with my family. Back then, I envisioned a perfectly planned path, with courses and mentors guiding every step. (Spoiler alert: that's not how it went!)

The real turning point came in 2019. With almost no savings and no online income, we booked one-way business class tickets Singapore to Istanbul (we used frequent flyer miles). We were fuelled by faith and a burning desire to break free.

Now, over five years later, we're still living our dream. We're a family of six, living a life of freedom, travel, and financial autonomy. Through unexpected challenges and constant personal growth, we've learned a powerful lesson: fear is a terrible travel companion.
