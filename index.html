From Dream to Reality

Hi there! I'm Melissa, the founder of How to Fund Travel. Buckle up, because my journey to get here has been anything but ordinary.

It all started with a dream: selling everything, traveling the world, and living a location-independent life with my family. Back then, I envisioned a perfectly planned path, with courses and mentors guiding every step. (Spoiler alert: that's not how it went!)

The real turning point came in 2019. With zero dollars in savings and no online income, we booked one-way business class tickets Singapore to Istanbul. We were fuelled by faith and a burning desire to break free.

Now, over four years later, we're still living our dream – a life of freedom, travel, and financial autonomy. Through unexpected challenges and constant personal growth, we've learned a powerful lesson: fear is a terrible travel companion.

Why Am I Sharing This?

Because if you're looking for someone who's actually living the life you crave, you've come to the right place. My mission is to help families like yours ditch the status quo and design a life that fuels your soul.

Here's the thing: fancy coaches with their picture-perfect lives might not resonate with you if your dream doesn't involve suburbia and keeping up with the Joneses. I believe in finding a mentor who aligns with your values and aspirations.

This website is for rebels, dream seekers, and anyone who knows there's more to life than the ordinary. I'm here to guide you through the process of deconditioning, ditching old patterns, and embracing a life of wild, free adventure (families included).
