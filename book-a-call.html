Frequently Asked Questions

Is this consultation really free?

Yes! This consultation is completely free. I believe in providing value upfront and helping families understand their options before making any commitments.

What if I'm not ready to start traveling yet?

That's perfectly fine! Many families start planning years in advance. We can discuss your timeline and create a roadmap for achieving your goals, whether you want to start next month or in a few years.

Do I need to prepare anything?

No formal preparation required, but it helps to have a general idea of your travel goals and current situation. The more specific you can be about your dreams, the more targeted advice I can provide.

What if I need more help after our call?

During our consultation, I'll let you know about additional resources, courses, or coaching options that might be helpful for your specific situation. There's no pressure to purchase anything.
