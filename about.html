About Me

My Journey to Location Independence

Hi, I'm Melissa

For years, I dreamed of a life filled with travel and freedom. I envisioned our family living location-independently, supported by a fully remote income. After years of seeking the perfect formula through courses and mentors, the key turned out to be a leap of faith.

In 2019, with very little savings or stable online income, we booked one-way tickets and embraced the unknown. This belief in creating our own path, along with some unexpected twists, has led us to an incredible journey of travel, personal growth, and financial autonomy.

I've created this blog to share our experiences since we set off in February 2020. Here, I document the various ways we've tried (and often failed) to fund our travels. Explore the strategies we discovered, and hopefully, you'll find something that resonates and works for you and your family.

Why I Share This Journey

Because if you're looking for someone who's actually living the life you crave, you've come to the right place. My mission is to help families like yours ditch the status quo and design a life that fuels your soul.

Here's the thing: fancy coaches with their picture-perfect lives might not resonate with you if your dream doesn't involve suburbia and keeping up with the Joneses. I believe in finding a mentor who aligns with your values and aspirations.

This website is for rebels, dream seekers, and anyone who knows there's more to life than the ordinary. I'm here to guide you through the process of deconditioning, ditching old patterns, and embracing a life of wild, free adventure (families included).
